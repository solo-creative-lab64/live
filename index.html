Website summary

BhoomDom is a cinema-focused platform for Indian film fans. It combines movie reviews, trending recommendations, OTT streaming highlights, subscription sharing, and ticket marketplace features in one portal. The site promotes regional and mainstream cinema, premium membership access, and easy discovery of movies across languages.

  • Trending movie recommendations and OTT highlights
  • OTT sharing groups for Netflix, Hotstar, and others
  • Ticket marketplace for verified cinema seats
  • Pro subscription with ad-free browsing and early reviews
